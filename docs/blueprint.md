# **App Name**: Series Tracker

## Core Features:

- Show Search and Discovery: Allow users to search for TV shows by title, genre, or keyword.
- Watchlist: Enable users to add TV shows to a personal watchlist to track progress.
- Episode Tracking: Allow users to mark episodes as watched and track their progress within a show.
- User Reviews and Ratings: Enable users to write reviews and rate TV shows.
- Recommendations: Suggest TV shows to users based on their viewing history and ratings using an AI tool.
- Social Sharing: Allow users to share their watchlist, reviews, and ratings with friends.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep indigo (#4B0082) to evoke a sense of immersion and quality content.
- Background color: Very dark gray (#222222), nearly black, to ensure maximum contrast with content and promote a cinematic feel.
- Accent color: Electric purple (#BF00FF), analogous to indigo but much brighter, to highlight interactive elements.
- Font pairing: 'Playfair' (serif) for headlines to convey sophistication, 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body to ensure readability.
- Use simple, line-based icons for navigation and actions.
- Emphasize visual hierarchy to present content clearly. Utilize card-based layouts to highlight individual TV shows.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience.
